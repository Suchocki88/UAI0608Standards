Understanding AI: A Conceptual Approach
Chapter 6, Lesson 6.8: You Know More Than You Think (Discussion Day)

Lesson 6.8 is a Discussion Day focused on consolidation through articulation. Students take inventory of what they can explain about Chapters 5 and 6 concepts without notes, then select one concept to explain to a partner. After drafting a written explanation, students hide their notes and practice explaining orally while partners interrupt with clarifying questions. This calibration process reveals gaps between internal understanding and external articulation. Students then revise their explanations based on discovered gaps. Class discussion surfaces patterns about the articulation experience itself, and field journal reflections capture the meta-learning. The lesson prepares students for the Unit C exam while teaching transferable study skills grounded in learning science (retrieval practice, generation, calibration). The celebration framing creates psychological safety for productive struggle.
